Welcome to the 3D Diorama Background Demo

This demo shows a diorama background with 3D rotation and perspective effects. Use the controls on the right to experiment with different 3D transformations.

The 3D effects create depth staging where layers at different z-indices appear at different depths, creating a realistic parallax effect when rotated.
